Maze of Space - Release 1

The game took somewhere around 2 weeks to do
Check Maze of Space's website for new releases and other info.


Instructions

A game about killing space monsters.

You're trapped on a space station.

[Arrow keys] Move the guy around.
[CTRL] or [Z] Fires your gun.
[ALT] or [X] Strafes.
[I] for inventory.
